Vehicle

Dec 12th, 2014 6:15 am

I previously covered ‘driving’ (http://everzast.com/blog/drive-like-jason/) after the Apoc, but what should you be driving like Jason?

Learn Everything

First, you need to make sure you can drive almost anything:

  • Stick Shift? Check!
  • Motorcycle? Check!
  • Bus? Check!
  • Bike? Check!
  • Plane? …
  • Helicopter? …

Fortunately the last two aren’t ‘driving’ but they are still a gap for me. I should be able to fly a plane. So maybe eventually.

OK, now that you can drive everything. You should be driving whatever is drivable. There might be better things, but if you have a fueled vehicle, you are in good shape. Zombies are definitely slower than most vehicles out there.

Better

Now if you happen to have a few choices on vehicles, there are definitely better vehicles than other vehicles. The critical things you are looking for are:

  • Fuel Type
  • Speed!
  • Momentum!
  • Flexibility!
  • Range!

Pretty much, the first factor is the category of the vehicle (Gas, Electric, etc.) and after you pick that, you need to maximize the rest of the numbers. Ideally you cover multiple fuel types and maximize all the rest.

Strangely the universe has set me up to have the perfect combination of vehicles. Again, I need to be careful of what I write about because it seems to come true.

My current vehicle garage looks like this:

  • Scion TC. Gas. Good Mileage. Fast-ish. Stealth-ish. About 250 mile range. Front-wheel drive.
  • Range Rover HSE. Gas. Bad Mileage. Can go through three feet of water. About 300 mile range. Four-wheel drive.
  • Tesla P85D. Electric. Awesome Mileage. Awesome speed. 0-60 in <4 seconds. About 300 mile range. Both gas and solar refuel. All-wheel drive.
  • 2x Zero FX Motorcycle. Electric. Awesome mileage. Awesome speed. 0-60 in some crazy number. About 75 mile range. Both gas and solar refuel.

Several of the above I do not yet own, but that appears to be what my garage is meant to look like. I am pretty sure that has most Apoc scenarios covered… Gas and Electric. Fast on road or able to be off-road. Four and two wheel variants.

I do love my Scion, but all the crazy-awesome vehicles beneath it make the Scion even better. And make my family and community safer.
