Safety!

Dec 5th, 2014 6:15 am

Continuing further into the jungle… no safetys! If I draw a gun, I am about to kill you. Make your choice, you are opposed by lethal force. I drew a gun. Lethal (life ending in a split of a second) force. If you wish to live, please move along. I do not want to kill you. But if you do not move along, I will. Simple choice. If you look at my shot pattern, you will recognize that this is not a variable. I spray within a centimeter. Yes. Seriously. I am a professional shooter. I was taught by myself and a professional mage name Rick. I “don’t miss”. Kill me or move along. The third option neither you or I will enjoy.

It is all about the revolvers!

So the dynamics of revolvers are very much more interesting than automatics. If you have a great automatic, you have a great ‘touch’ weapon. Touch it. People die. Or targets get nasty holes. A revolver has two epic choices. Flow through the trigger, or ‘touch’ it. If you do not cock (with your other hand, fool!) the revolver, the flow is incredibly satisfying. If you precock the instrument-of-destruction, then things are simpler. The first feels like aikido, the second like the god of death. Interesting alternatives.
