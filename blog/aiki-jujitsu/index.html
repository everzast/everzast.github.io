Aiki Jujitsu

Feb 1st, 2015 6:15 am

You need to be always armed if there are zombies floating around. Zombies are rarely fast, but they can be stealthy as they wander around… and some can hide in the ground… behind trees. You need to be ready and able to respond to a sudden threat appearing.

Ideally you are heavily armed and can dispose of the threat quickly, but if you are caught without a significant weapon, you need to fight hand-to-hand. As described elsewhere, a number of fighting arts from Japan have been demilitarized postwar. You do not want to learn to thumb-lock a zombie. So although beautiful, something like Aikido is not going to do you well. Throwing zombie with Judo is also not something you want to do day-to-day although I admit combining it with some bowling pins makes it sound pretty fun.

No, you want each interaction to end quickly and hopefully permanently. I have not studied Zombie anatomy enough to be sure, but I think Aiki Jujitsu may be one of the better anti-zombie martial arts sans weapons.
