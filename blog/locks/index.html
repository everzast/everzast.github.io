Locks!

Dec 10th, 2014 6:15 am

I wrote before about safety and how I don’t want a safety on my guns. That is because a safety is a delusion. A drawn weapon is not safe, so don’t try to make it safer. That is the dull-knife mentality. I understand if you must carry a loaded gun, a mechanical safety might be useful. Better would be a grip lock that recognized your hand. And I can come up with better than that too because I am ridiculously creative nowadays.

But I don’t have to carry a loaded gun, so I can simply ‘lock it away’. Hence no safety. Must undo locks and after that, clearly I intend to use the nasty thing or I would relock it.

Ultimate Lock

The ultimate lock is the thing simply does not exist… or at least not to you. My F-14 is very safely locked away. As are all my ICBMs. Nuclear subs. Etc.

Multiple Locks

Even with the ultimate lock, it is useful to have multiple locks on things. This isn’t redundancy: you can’t get in with just one of the two locks… you must get both of them.

For example, my F-14 has multiple locks. If you park it in front of my house, ready for me to jump in, it is unfortunately still locked despite it’s proximity. It is actually ridiculously locked:

  • It may have no fuel, and I can’t tow an F-14 to a fighter-jet-fueling-station!
  • Even if I could, I don’t know where a FJFS is!
  • Even if I could, I don’t think they would let me fuel it (being a civilian and all)!
  • Even if I could, I don’t think Moffet would let me pull it to their runway (civilian again) and Palo Alto might be too short!
  • EIIC: I don’t have a helmet… but maybe you left that in there…
  • EIIC: I don’t actually know how to fly an F-14. So I better get a video game or mage to teach me.

So my F-14 has at least seven locks on it. Very unlikely I am going to accidentally get access to it. Which suits me fine: I don’t think I will need my F-14 any time soon. And if I do (Zombies and all), I think I can survive long enough to undo the seven locks.

Weapon locks

Turns out all my weapons are locked pretty similarly to the F-14. I simply don’t own any of them. So undoing the locks will take quite a bit of forethought and a significant passage of time. Much better and safer than a stupid safety :-)

I do carry seven tennis rackets though…
