Zombie Gear!

Dec 16th, 2014 6:15 am

This post contains other gear you might want and other sites that discuss preparation. It started when I encountered ‘ZAPS’ while reading through the web.

  • http://www.zapsgear.com

In terms of other gear, you can divide it into a few different categories:

  • Individual Survival and Effectiveness
    • Proper winter clothing, shoes, hats, gloves (winter and working), maybe swimming needs, etc. This you can mostly get from REI, Sports Basement, and similar good camping and gear stores
  • Group Survival
    • Tents, water conversions, vehicles capable of moving groups of people, packs able to transport water and other devices, water purifiers, etc.
    • Food acquisition tools: fishing, hunting, foraging, and hunting.
    • Note a lot of these would help the individual, but you should probably be thinking “group” when getting them. A one-person tent is nice but not that helpful for groups.
    • Again, REI and similar stores are a good source
  • Defense gear
    • This could be traps, fences, and the like. Or defensive weaponry.
  • Offensive gear
    • This would be more serious weaponry that can ‘assault’ zombies who control important positions or buildings
    • This isn’t an area I am particularly concerned with
