Redundancy!

Strangely the second most important skill to “Freeing Your Mind” will be redundancy.

Redundancy is the art of having a “live” equivalent or “hot” spare to basically everything. If you have this, and one item fails (Zombie Alert Warning: ZAW), then the backup item (ZAW2) is incredibly unlikely to fail at exactly the same time. If ZAW1 and ZAW2 fail at exactly the same time, then you do no have a real ZAW at all.

Redundancy is incredibly valuable in all things. Except in marriage. Monogamy is clearly superior. But even then you should have a Hot Spare readily available. Because you never know do you? Zombies and all!
